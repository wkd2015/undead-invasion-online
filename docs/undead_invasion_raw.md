## Game background
Undead Invasion is a pixel-style, side-scrolling zombie battle survival game published by Azgames in 2024 for browser and mobile, with single-player survival wave-defense gameplay. [azgames](https://azgames.io/undead-invasion)

The premise is that you are a lone post-apocalyptic survivor facing wave after wave of zombies that become more intense each day, with no checkpoints if you die. [undead-invasion](https://www.undead-invasion.com)

## Core gameplay
The game mixes zombie shooting and defense mechanics, so you fight undead enemies while also building defenses to slow them down. [azgames](https://azgames.io/undead-invasion)

The loop is: survive a wave, earn coins from kills and from surviving the full day, then spend those coins on stronger weapons, health items, barricades, traps, and other defense items before the next wave. [undead-invasion](https://www.undead-invasion.com)

The source pages also describe the progression as increasingly aggressive zombie hordes with faster, stronger, and more varied zombie types as days go on. [azgames](https://azgames.io/undead-invasion)

## Wave progression
The game is explicitly described as wave-based survival, where each day brings increasingly aggressive zombie hordes. [undead-invasion](https://www.undead-invasion.com)

The pages state that the longer you survive, the more coins you earn, and those coins feed into gear upgrades for the next wave. [undead-invasion](https://www.undead-invasion.com)

If you die, you restart from the beginning because there are no checkpoints. [azgames](https://azgames.io/undead-invasion)

## Defense placement
The pages say barricades and traps should be placed before each wave or between waves, and that proper placement is crucial. [azgames](https://azgames.io/undead-invasion)

One guide page says traps should be placed before choke points or in narrow passages where many zombies pass through, and barricades should be used to slow zombies long enough for you to keep firing or repair. [undeadcorridor](https://undeadcorridor.io/undead-invasion)

The same page recommends using kicks when zombies get too close or touch barricades, to create space and keep the defensive line from being overrun. [undeadcorridor](https://undeadcorridor.io/undead-invasion)

## Survival strategy details
The repeated strategy advice is to maintain distance from zombies, use ranged weapons to thin them out, switch to melee or kicks when they close in, and save grenades for large clusters. [undeadcorridor](https://undeadcorridor.io/undead-invasion)

The guide-style pages also stress balancing offense and defense by combining weapons with barricades and traps instead of relying on only one method. [undeadcorridor](https://undeadcorridor.io/undead-invasion)

The only specific grenade advice found in the retrieved sources is to use grenades to eliminate large groups or clusters of zombies. [undead-invasion](https://www.undead-invasion.com)
